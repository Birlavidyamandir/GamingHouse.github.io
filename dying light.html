About Game

 Dying Light is a first-person, action survival game set in a vast open world. Roam a city
devastated by a mysterious epidemic, scavenging for supplies and crafting weapons to
help defeat the hordes of flesh-hungry enemies the plague has created. At night, beware
the Infected as they grow in strength and even more lethal nocturnal predators leave
their nests to feed on their prey.
