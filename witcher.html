About Game

 1. In the past he has raised and overthrown monarchs, battled legendary
monsters and saved the lives of many. Now Geralt embarks on his most
personal quest to save his loved ones and protect the world from an
ancient threat.

2. The story is drawn based on player decisions. Each action will have
consequences which change the story and the game world. NPCs,
communities, monsters and locations all change, based on player
choice.

3. The Witcher 3 is standalone adventure, easily entered into by new
players. Witcher fans will find subtle references to their adventures,
but these elements are not necessary to enjoy the game fully.

4. A breathtaking cinematic introduction demonstrating the game
background – this puts players immediately in the know about the
situation in the war-ravaged Northern Kingdoms and the background
story of the main character.

5. Unique atmosphere, memorable characters and gritty dialog – the
game world has its own unique feel. It is a classic dark fantasy tale that
will appeal to fans of all kinds of fantasy stories>
