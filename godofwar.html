About Game
1. A New Beginning – His vengeance against the gods of Olympus far
behind him, Kratos now lives as a man in the lands of Norse Gods and
monsters. It is in this harsh, unforgiving world that he must fight to
survive… and teach his son to do the same.

2. Vicious, Physical Combat – With an intimate, over-the-shoulder free
camera that brings the action closer than ever, combat in God of War is
up close, frenetic, and unflinching. Kratos’ axe —powerful, magic and
multi-faceted – is a brutal weapon as well as a versatile tool for exploration.

3. Midgard and Beyond – Set within the untamed forests, mountains, and
realms of Norse lore, God of War features a distinctly new setting with
its own pantheon of creatures, monsters, and gods.
